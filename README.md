📌 Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard analyzes smoking-related health risk patterns to support data-driven decision-making in a healthcare or public health context. The report visualizes key risk indicators, demographic trends, and behavioral patterns to help stakeholders identify high-risk population segments and prioritize intervention strategies.

🧠 Business Problem
Healthcare organizations and public health teams need clear visibility into smoking risk factors across different demographics to:

Identify high-risk population groups
Allocate resources and interventions effectively
Track behavioral trends over time
Support evidence-based policy decisions
